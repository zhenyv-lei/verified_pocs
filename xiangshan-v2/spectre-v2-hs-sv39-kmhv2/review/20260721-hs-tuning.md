   - `PROBE_CANDIDATES=16` covers `'S'` and produced a successful one-byte leak.

## Current conclusion

The HS Spectre-v2 SV39 PoC is not blocked at the basic trap/victim/probe control path for a one-byte, one-try configuration. With `PROBE_CANDIDATES=16`, it leaks the first byte `S` correctly. The dominant confounder is runtime: even minimal one-byte cells take about 4–6 minutes on the current `XiangShan/build/emu`, and UART printing is slow enough to truncate logs if the emulator is killed immediately after the first result marker.

For full-string or larger candidate matrices, use longer per-cell budgets and prefer progress markers/status files over short fixed `timeout` windows.
